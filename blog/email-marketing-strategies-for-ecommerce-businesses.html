Email Marketing Strategies for Ecommerce Businesses

30 Dec 20256 min read
Home / Blog / Email Marketing Strategies for Ecommerce Businesses
🛒

Email remains one of the highest-return marketing channels for ecommerce, particularly for repeat purchases from an existing customer base.

Automate abandoned cart emails

These are typically the highest-converting automated emails a store can set up, recovering otherwise-lost sales.

Segment by purchase behaviour

First-time buyers, repeat customers and lapsed customers respond to different messaging — avoid one-size-fits-all campaigns.

Keep a consistent sending schedule

Irregular sending hurts engagement over time — a predictable, moderate frequency performs better than sporadic bursts.

A well-run email programme often outperforms paid advertising on cost-per-sale once a reasonable list size is built.
