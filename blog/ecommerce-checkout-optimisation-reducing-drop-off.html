Ecommerce Checkout Optimisation: Reducing Drop-Off

14 Jan 20266 min read
Home / Blog / Ecommerce Checkout Optimisation: Reducing Drop-Off
🛒

Checkout is where the most valuable traffic on the entire site exists — customers who already decided to buy — so drop-off here is the costliest to lose.

Show a progress indicator

Customers are more likely to complete a multi-step checkout when they can see how many steps remain.

Avoid surprise costs

Display shipping and any fees as early as possible, ideally before the final payment step.

Offer guest checkout

Requiring account creation before purchase is one of the most common reasons customers abandon at this stage.

Every field and step removed from checkout is one less opportunity for a customer to give up partway through.
