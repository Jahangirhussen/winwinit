Typography Basics for Non-Designers

05 Nov 20255 min read
Home / Blog / Typography Basics for Non-Designers
🎨

Typography choices affect readability and perceived professionalism more than most business owners realise, and a few basic rules avoid the most common mistakes.

Limit yourself to two typefaces

One for headings, one for body text — more than that usually looks chaotic rather than intentional.

Prioritise readability over style

A beautiful font that is hard to read at small sizes will frustrate readers regardless of how it looks.

Use consistent hierarchy

Headings, subheadings and body text should be clearly distinguished by size and weight, applied consistently throughout.

Good typography is often invisible — readers simply absorb the content without friction, which is exactly the point.
