Building Customer Trust on a New Online Store

19 Jan 20265 min read
Home / Blog / Building Customer Trust on a New Online Store
🛒

New stores without an established reputation need to actively build trust signals that established brands get for free.

Show real contact information

A visible phone number, email and physical address makes a store feel like a real business rather than anonymous.

Display clear policies upfront

Shipping times, returns and refund policy should be easy to find, not buried in fine print.

Collect and display early reviews

Even a handful of genuine reviews meaningfully increases buyer confidence for a new store.

Trust is built through transparency — the more clearly a store communicates what to expect, the more confident customers feel buying.
