FAQ

Ecommerce Website FAQs

What's the difference between single and multi-vendor?+

A single-vendor store sells only your own products. A multi-vendor marketplace lets multiple sellers list and manage their own products under one platform.

Which payment gateways do you support?+

We commonly integrate Stripe and PayPal, and can integrate other UK-supported gateways depending on your requirements.

Can the store handle a large product catalogue?+

Yes, our ecommerce builds are designed to scale — from a few dozen products to catalogues with thousands of SKUs.
