1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function correctly, remember your preferences, and understand how visitors use the site.

2. Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies — required for core site functionality such as navigation and remembering your cookie preferences. The site cannot function properly without these.
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors use the site, so we can improve it (used only if analytics tools such as Google Analytics are added to this site).
  • Preference cookies — remember choices you make (such as newsletter sign-up) so you don't need to re-enter them.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Embedded content such as the Google Map on our Contact page may set its own cookies in line with the relevant third party's own cookie and privacy policy.

4. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers let you control cookies through their settings, including blocking or deleting them. Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect how this website functions. You can manage cookie settings in your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions
