Our Promise

We want every client to feel confident working with WINWINIT. If we fail to deliver what was agreed at the start of your project, you're entitled to a refund under the terms below.

What's Covered

  • If work has not started on your project within the agreed timeframe, you can request a full refund.
  • If the final delivered website or design does not match the agreed scope and we're unable to fix it after reasonable revision attempts, you can request a partial or full refund depending on work completed.
  • Deposits are refundable in full if cancelled before design or development work begins.

What's Not Covered

  • Change of mind after work has been completed as agreed in the original scope.
  • Third-party costs already spent on your behalf, such as domain names, hosting or premium plugins/themes.
  • Delays caused by late feedback, content or approvals from your side.

How to Request a Refund

Contact us at hello@winwinit.co.uk with your order details and reason for the request. We aim to respond within 2 business days and resolve valid requests within 7 business days.
