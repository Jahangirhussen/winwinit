FAQ

WooCommerce Store FAQs

How is WooCommerce different from Shopify?+

WooCommerce runs on your own WordPress site, so you own everything outright with no monthly platform fee — Shopify is a hosted platform with its own subscription. We can advise which suits your business.

Do I need hosting for a WooCommerce store?+

Yes, WooCommerce needs WordPress hosting. We can recommend reliable UK hosting providers suited to your store's size, billed separately at cost.

Can you migrate my existing store to WooCommerce?+

Yes, we can migrate products, orders and customers from most platforms into a new WooCommerce store.
