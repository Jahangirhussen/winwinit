Getting Started

Working With WINWINIT

What services does WINWINIT offer?+

We build WordPress websites, Shopify stores and ecommerce platforms, and provide graphics design services including logos, branding and marketing creative.

How do I get started with a project?+

Fill in the contact form or send us a message on WhatsApp with a brief description of what you need. We'll reply within one business day to arrange a free consultation call.

Do you only work with UK businesses?+

Most of our clients are based in the UK, but we work remotely with clients internationally via video calls, email and project management tools.

Pricing & Process

Timelines, Pricing & Payment

How long does a typical project take?+

Most WordPress websites take 2–4 weeks, while Shopify and ecommerce builds typically take 4–8 weeks depending on scope. Enterprise projects with custom features can take longer.

What is included in the price?+

All quoted prices are in GBP and include design, development and testing for the agreed scope. Domain registration and hosting are billed separately at cost, unless otherwise agreed.

Do you require a deposit?+

Yes, we typically ask for a 50% deposit before starting work, with the remaining balance due on completion, before the site goes live.

Can I request a custom package?+

Of course. Every business is different — send us your requirements via the contact page and we'll prepare a tailored quote.

Support

After Launch

Do you offer support after launch?+

Yes, every package includes a period of free priority support after launch (30–90 days depending on the package), with ongoing maintenance plans available afterwards.

Will I be able to update the website myself?+

Yes. We build on WordPress and Shopify specifically so you can manage content, products and pages yourself, and we provide a short handover walkthrough at launch.

What happens if something breaks after launch?+

Get in touch and we'll investigate. Fixes covered by your support period are free; anything outside that scope is quoted before we start.
